Service Our services include:

  • ✔ Aadhar & PAN Card Services

    Linking Aadhaar to PAN is mandatory for most individuals and must be done via the Income Tax e‑Filing portal. You can link even without logging in, using the “Link Aadhaar” quick link. If you’ve not linked by June 30, 2023, your PAN becomes inactive eshram.gov.in +15 Income Tax Department +15 Protean eGov Technologies +15 .

    How to link: Visit the Income Tax e‑Filing site, enter your PAN & Aadhaar, pay a fee if required, and authenticate via OTP .

    Troubleshooting: Ensure PAN and Aadhaar demographics (name, DOB, phone) match exactly. If mismatched, update details via UIDAI or NSDL/UTIITSL

  • ✔ Electricity, Water & Mobile Bill Payments

    Use platforms like Google Pay, PhonePe, Amazon Pay, or your bank’s online portal to pay local utility bills and mobile recharges. Set up auto-debit or manual payments for convenience.

  • ✔ Government Scheme Applications

    Use portals like myScheme or JanSamarth to discover and apply for Central government schemes aligned with your eligibility.

    For example, if you're a construction worker, you can apply for PM-SVANidhi via these portals.

  • ✔ Ration Card, E-Shram & Labour Registrations

    Ration Card: Apply or update through your respective state’s Food & Civil Supplies department website.

    e‑Shram Portal: A free registration platform for unorganized sector workers (age 16–59) to receive a Universal Account Number (UAN), access benefits, social security, and job aids

    How to register: Go to eshram.gov.in, fill in Aadhaar and bank details. Registration via CSCs/Seva Kendras or online

  • ✔ Online Form Filling & Document Printing

    Use online printing services (e.g., Printster, PrintOnWeb, Blinkit) that allow you to upload documents and get them printed/ delivered.

  • ✔ Mobile Recharge & Money Transfer

    Mobile recharge: Use apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Ding, Xoom for recharging Indian mobiles.

    Money transfers: Use Xoom, Western Union, or UPI for sending money both domestically and abroad.
